## Key Insights on Obesity
- The Western Pacific, Americas, and Eastern Mediterranean regions exhibit the highest obesity prevalence among all regions analyzed.
- Niue, Cook Islands, and Nauru rank as the countries with the highest obesity levels in the dataset.
- Female populations consistently show higher obesity rates over the years compared to males.

## Key Insights on Malnutrition
- Among all regions, South-East Asia and Africa report the lowest malnutrition prevalence, with India emerging as the country with the lowest malnutrition level.
- Bangladesh, Sri Lanka, Eritrea, and Ethiopia show comparable malnutrition prevalence levels in the dataset.
